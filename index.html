About me

Hi there! My name is Luxshie, but you can call me Lux. I was born and raised in Toronto, Ontario, and have never lived elsewhere.
That is until a few weeks ago, when I moved to Ottawa to begin the Interactive Media Design Program here at Algonquin.
It's been a smooth transition so far, and I am looking forward to what the next two years in a new city will bring!
I definetly feel like Ottawa is more my pace, and I also am enjoying being pushed out of my comfort zone a bit by moving here alone.
I am the youngest child of three, so I am used to being coddled by my caring, but at times overbearing, older siblings and parents.
I'm hopeful that this newfound independence will be worthwhile, and I might come out of this experience learning some new skills, like cooking.

Some of my interests outside of school include writing, reading and art. And if you haven't guessed already from that triad of hobbies,
I am an introvert. Creative writing is something i have been doing for as long as I can remember, but have only taken up more seriously in recent years.
I now spend much of my spare time working on poetry or short fiction pieces for grants that are offered by local magazines/companies.
While I enjoy painting, it's not something i've done much of over the past year, and I am hoping to get back into it now that I'm in Ottawa.
My favourite TV show is and will likely always be One Tree Hill, and I have the 23 Scott Jersey to prove it.
